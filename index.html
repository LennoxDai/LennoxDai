About

I am an incoming PhD student at Fudan University and the Shanghai AI Laboratory, advised by Prof. Yuhang Zang. I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU), where I was a member of the Luo Lab.

I also spent time as a Research Assistant at Westlake University LINS Lab (2025), an exchange student at UC Berkeley (2024), and an intern at 4Paradigm and Merit Interactive.

My research focuses on Natural Language Processing and Multimodal Learning — building AI systems that understand and reason across language and vision.
